UM Sustainability Days 2026
A university-wide celebration of sustainability across all UM Green Teams: workshops, activities, and events at every faculty.

FPN Green Team · April 2026

Event Recap

Tue · 28 April
Tote-Bag Painting

Participants painted and decorated their own tote bags: A creative and eco-friendly take-home from the Sustainability Days!

Outdoor Yoga

A calm outdoor yoga session in the fresh spring air, bringing students together to unwind and reconnect with nature right on campus.

Wed · 29 April
Veggie Get Together

A relaxed outdoor lunch bringing students together over plant-based food. A lovely afternoon of good conversation and good vibes.

Glass Painting

Students got creative decorating glass jars and bottles, turning used objects into recycled treasures.

Hiking Sint Pietersberg

A guided hike through the beautiful Sint Pietersberg nature reserve, just outside Maastricht. A perfect way to celebrate Earth and the outdoors.

Thu · 30 April
Planting & DIY Pots

Participants planted seedlings and decorated their own pots to take home.

Flea Market

A cute second-hand market at the Sustainability Hub, with students and staff buying, selling, and swapping pre-loved items. It really was the perfect closing event for Sustainability Days.

Thank you to everyone who joined us for Sustainability Days 2026! Follow us on Instagram @fpn_greenteam to stay up to date on future events.
